About Youth Forum

Thank you for your interest in participating in "Youth Forum", the premier forum for discussion for young changemakers to influence the leaders of tomorrow! The legal landscape is evolving, and young professionals play a pivotal role in shaping its future.

Our Objectives

  • To facilitate a direct line of communication between young legal professionals and Hon. Sajith Premadasa, offering a platform to voice ideas, concerns, and proposals on legal and policy reforms.
  • To enable participants to influence policy development by presenting their perspectives on legal reforms that can drive the nation's progress.
  • To build connections between young legal minds and influential policymakers, opening pathways for collaboration and mentorship.
    • Call to Action

    Young legal professionals and law students are encouraged to attend, engage, and be a part of this transformative discussion. Your voice matters in shaping the future of Sri Lanka’s legal system. Don’t miss this opportunity to contribute to a pivotal conversation that could influence the nation’s path forward.
